Welcome to the Border Highlanders Pipes & Drums! For over five decades, we have been proudly bringing the rich traditions of Scottish Highland music to the Palouse region. Our band celebrates the heritage of Scotland through the stirring sounds of bagpipes and the thundering rhythm of drums.
